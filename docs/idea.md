# Project Idea – AI Powered Insurance for Food Delivery Workers

## Persona
Food delivery partners working for platforms like Swiggy and Zomato.

These workers earn daily income through deliveries. External disruptions such as heavy rain, extreme heat, or severe pollution can stop deliveries and cause income loss.

Our platform provides parametric insurance that automatically compensates workers when such disruptions occur.

## Key Disruptions (Triggers)

1. Heavy Rain
Trigger: Rainfall above threshold (example: >80mm)

2. Extreme Heat
Trigger: Temperature above safe working level (example: >42°C)

3. Severe Pollution
Trigger: AQI above safe limit (example: AQI >400)

4. Flood Warning
Trigger: Government or weather API flood alert

## Weekly Pricing Model

Gig workers operate weekly, so the insurance policy uses a weekly premium model.

Example:

Low Risk Area  
Premium: ₹20/week  
Coverage: ₹800

Medium Risk Area  
Premium: ₹40/week  
Coverage: ₹1500

High Risk Area  
Premium: ₹70/week  
Coverage: ₹2500

AI calculates premiums using:
- weather history
- pollution levels
- disruption frequency

## AI Integration

AI will be used for:

1. Risk Assessment  
Predict risk level of worker's location.

2. Dynamic Premium Calculation  
Adjust weekly premium based on environmental risk.

3. Fraud Detection  
Detect anomalies like:
- GPS spoofing
- duplicate claims
- fake disruption claims

## Expected Outcome

The system ensures delivery workers receive automatic financial protection when external disruptions prevent them from working.
